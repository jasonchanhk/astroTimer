Work at night after work?

Persistence is the key to success. We appreciated your hard work on self-learning after a day of tiring work and commute. This could be your haven to feel you are becoming a better person day by day.

How does it work?

We assume you get to this page after dinner, resting a bit and ready to spend your night in a meaningful way. This clock is running a 50/10 timer, after doing what you want to persist for 50 minutes there will be a 10 minutes break. Do come back afterward because this clock last for 3 cycle, afterward the clock will automatically turn off.

Why setting a limit?

We believe you should NOT overworking. Although you spent your precious day time on work, you should not be vengeful studying whenever there is time. Trust me, you will get exhausted and drain out quickly after few nights of rushing. It may risk your day time job and once you give up shortly, it may take more time for you to pick up.

Those who use this clock should be one who possess strong desire for improvement but also a peace of mind. You will grow slow and steady with us, reaching your goal in a countable way. So please, be sure you enjoy the short night.
